**YOUTHX** (YouthX is Open Useful Technologies Hub’s Experiments) is a collective dedicated to ethical AI research, experimentation, and the creation of open technologies. We believe that technology must remain free and accessible: if it is open, it is transparent; if it is open, it belongs to everyone.

## Our Mission

We conduct research in artificial intelligence, develop our own models, and publish our findings openly to contribute to a culture of shared knowledge. Our mission is to push the boundaries of technology while keeping freedom, ethics, and accessibility at the core of everything we do.

## What We Do

From developing AI models to writing scientific articles, we aim to contribute meaningful research and practical technologies. Our work includes building open tools, publishing ethical frameworks, and fostering discussions about the future of artificial intelligence.
